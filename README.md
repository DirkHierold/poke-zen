# 🌌 SoulLock

> **"Lock your soul into deep focus. One stray swipe, and everything shatters."**

SoulLock is a brutal, gamified mindfulness timer designed to break your digital dopamine addiction. It forces you into a state of absolute presence by punishing even the slightest distraction. 

Unlike traditional productivity apps that gently remind you to stay focused, SoulLock enforces strict discipline: **If you leave the tab, switch apps, or check a notification, your progress is instantly and permanently destroyed.**

---

## 🔮 The Core Principle: Brutal Zen

SoulLock uses the smartphone's browser limitations as a game mechanic. iOS naturally freezes background JavaScript tasks to save energy. SoulLock monitors this behavior to detect when you stray from your path of mindfulness.

* **The Soul Aura:** A glowing, meditative circle expands as long as you maintain your focus.
* **The Permadeath Trigger:** Swiping up, switching to WhatsApp, or tapping a notification banner instantly cracks the aura, resulting in a permanent fail.
* **The Reward:** Complete the full countdown to successfully lock in your focus session and manifest your reward.

---

## ⚙️ Features

- 🧘 **True Mindfulness:** Designed to be looked at *out of the corner of your eye* or left flat on the table.
- 🚫 **Zero Tolerance (Visibility API):** Instantly detects when the browser tab loses focus.
- 🕒 **Time-Warp Protection:** Uses real-time system clock verification. Even if you quickly switch back, it detects the missing seconds and triggers a reset.
- 🕯️ **Screen Wake Lock:** Prevents your phone from naturally going to sleep, ensuring you are the one actively choosing to stay or leave.
- 📱 **PWA Ready:** Can be added directly to your iPhone Home Screen via Safari for an immersive, full-screen app experience without browser bars.

---

## 🚀 Quick Start (Play It Now)

1. Open the website on your smartphone (preferably via Safari on iOS).
2. Tap the **Share button** (the square with an arrow pointing up).
3. Select **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4. Launch **SoulLock** from your home screen like a native app.
5. Set your timer, start the ritual, and **do not touch your phone**.
